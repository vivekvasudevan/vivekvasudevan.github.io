Jinance - An Australian Cryptocurrency exchange

My Role

  • UX/UI Designer
  • Delegate work to other designer
  • Lockdown and finalize designs

My Deliverable

Responsible for the competitive research of different exchange platforms, conduct user interviews, create user personas, information architecture + wireframes for the website, clickable prototype and final presentation

Tools

Problem

The exchange platform did not clearly communicate what it was for neither did the home page drive the users to trade

The landing page needed a huge overhaul and needed to convert more people visiting the page. In my role as a UX Designer, I worked with the CEO, BAs & Marketing team to further discuss these pain points and possible solutions. As the project continued I met with them regularly. I made sure to inform them of my progress and share any research I conducted.

Understand

Research and analyze different exchange platforms (competitors) noting everything they do to organize their site.
Conduct 1 on 1 interviews with people who attend the blockchain meetups and friends working with financial institutions.
Gain knowledge of best practices for website design. I learned about heuristics evaluations, usability tests, and scannability.

Key takeaways from the research and interviews

  • Most of the users could not understand what Jinance was by looking at the landing page.
  • There was no information about the people behind the exchange platform.
  • There was no call to action for the main purpose of the site.
  • The important information was on the secondary and tertiary levels of the website & the content was not organized.
  • They spoke about the limited time they had to understand an exchange website and the need to access information quickly and easily.

Define

Casual Trader

"Just for fun" Justin

Age: 25–30

Location: Penrith NSW

Family Status: Single

Career/Industry: Sales Rep – tech

Education: Undergraduate – IT

Goals
He's a tech-savvy sales rep who's starting to save money and wants to get into cryptocurrency trading to build a healthy financial safety net and buy a property in the near future.
Motivators
He wants to trade digital currencies because of the growing crypto market and high return potentials. He sees blockchain and the crypto market as the investing tools of the future so he wants to get in early.
Challenges
Inexperienced investor, has a fear of the unpredictable market but understands that some risks need to be taken. Does not trust the unregulated crypto market. Concerned about exchange provider reputation. Fears losing his money to scammy platforms. Wants someone to hold his hand while he gets started, so customer service is important to him. There is a lot of information online, he doesn't want to be bombarded with information.
Sources of information
Social: Facebook, LinkedIn, Google, Telegram. Online channels: CoinTelegraf, CoinMarketCap, and BitcoinTalk.

Frequent Trader

"Frequent Freddie"

Age: 30–40

Location: Newcastle NSW

Family Status: Married, three kids

Career/Industry: FMCG Marketing Director

Education: Bachelor of Business

Goals
Freddie knows that the sector is growing and wants to be at the forefront of it. He likes the idea of trading digital currency because he knows it can be highly profitable, he wants to be financially independent before retiring. He can't risk losing his crypto investments so security is very important.
Motivators
He's been involved for several years and has already managed to earn a fair chunk. He's a true blockchain supporter who believes in the cryptocurrency idea itself and promotes it in every possible way.
Challenges
Busy lifestyle and family commitments make him unable to spend too much time on the platform. He needs an exchange that is not only the most affordable, but also easy to use, fast and trustworthy. He knows about other platforms rewards programs, so he wants the best possible deals.
Sources of information
Social media groups, Online crypto channels.

Advanced Trader

"Sophisticated Sam"

Age: 30–45

Location: Melbourne VIC

Family Status: Married, small family

Career/Industry: Management Level

Education: Bachelor of IT / MBA

Goals
His income now rests on his trading success. Sam's objective is to achieve a comfortable lifestyle, financially free to spend more time doing the things important to him. He wants to be in control of his own time.
Motivators
He made a fair bit of money trading the stock market and cryptocurrency. He seeks a connection to the world of crypto trading and would relish any social opportunity and wants to remain in the know. Industry updates in the form of reports are important to him.
Challenges
Busy lifestyle and with a family to support, risk has to be monitored. Due to his previous experience, his knowledge is extensive and he's quite set in his own ways. Changing platform would be hard for him, he's loyal to his current one.
Sources of information
Social media groups, Online crypto channels, industry reports.

Ideate

I had a brainstorming session with my colleague and came with rough designs for the new look of the website. I made a paper prototype of the home page and tested it out with a few of my colleagues to see if it was having the desired result.

After this I created wireframes for the new landing page. The wireframes covered the key sections including the hero, navigation, features, trading information, and the call to action. I used both Sketch and Adobe XD throughout the process to explore layout options and iterate quickly based on feedback from the team and stakeholders. There have been many revisions to the design and the website has come a long way from the original design.
