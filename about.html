About Me

I'm a product designer and design leader who turns complex problems into clear, scalable experiences.

 

I began my career in the game industry as a game designer, where I learned how systems thinking, behavioural psychology, and intuitive interactions shape engagement. That foundation continues to influence how I approach product design today, balancing creativity with structure, and vision with execution.

Over the years, I've led end-to-end design across AI-powered platforms, SaaS ecosystems, and mobile applications. I thrive at the intersection of product strategy and user experience, aligning business goals, technology, and human needs to build solutions that are not only usable, but impactful.

 

For me, design is about clarity, momentum, and measurable outcomes. I enjoy leading teams, mentoring designers, and collaborating cross-functionally to ship products that scale and genuinely serve their users.
